Norfolf Island

Norfolk Island is the Pacific's best kept secret. It is an island richly steeped in history and brimming with a people whom are passionately proud about their culture. The island may be small, but you certainly will be awestruck how much paradise has waiting to offer you.

Norfolk Island is waiting for you to rediscover her. The best part is, it's only a short 2.5 hour flight away from Christchurch. Whether you want adventure, recreation, relaxation, history, culture, nature or discovery, Norfolk Island has just a little bit of everything you've been looking for.
