Places To Eat

Restaurants dinning, fish fry, bbq Places to eat on Norfolk Island are only restricted by the imagination. The restaurant is teeming with neat little niche eateries and cafes and one will be spoiled for eating options while staying on the Island.


Grabbing some good old fashioned fish 'n' chips and sitting on the white sandy beach at Emily Bay is a popular option which locals will quickly get you hooked onto. Other than the Island's famous fish frys, another popular option is to go to the local supermarket and buy a roasted chook and a baguette. Chickens live a feral existence on the island and it is this wild carefree life which adds to their taste.

Visits to local houses for high teas is an enchanting way to dine while staying in Norfolk. There is nothing quite like sipping tea from classical bone china sets while enjoying strawberry jam and cream ladden scones. There are also some wonderful small artisan food producers on the island, including cheese makers and liqueur producers. The Islanders are proud of their produce and you will find many local businesses inviting people to come in and sample their edible crafts. There are several bars and clubs with breathtaking views around the Island where you can enjoy your favourite drop from back in Australia or New Zealand. Or, you could try something special and off the beaten track conjured up the Islanders themselves.
