Places To Stay

There are so many options when it comes to staying on Norfolk Island. For those that want to experience the Island like an Islander the true way to go is to live with a local. There are a number of bed and breakfasts on Norfolk Island which add a uniquely personal touch to visiting their Island.


For those seeking elegance and a touch of class, there is an excellent hotel on the island while for the budget conscious there are a superb variety of motels and self-contained apartments that also often provide a car to their guests. Al aternative to the bed and breakfast experience would be to spend your time in a classic Norfolk Island cottage or villa. These are historic homes with have been impeccably preseverved and capture a rare beauty of those whom lived a more privileged existence on the Island.

The best time to visit Norfolk Island comes down to you! The most busiest period is during the Spring and in the Summer. However, even during the supposedly cooler months, Norfolk's a surprisingly pleasant place to stay year round. Accommodation can be catered around an interest. One may wish to stay in a place close to the historical sites or perhaps close to a recreational zone. Accommodation on the Island will cater for those seeking adventure, discovery or those whom simply want basic accommodation with a kitchenette for the budget travellers to those whom wanting a pad with a swimming pool and a BBQ area for the family.


The choice is only limited by the imagination. The Islanders pride themselves on their ability to cater for any intredid explorer.
